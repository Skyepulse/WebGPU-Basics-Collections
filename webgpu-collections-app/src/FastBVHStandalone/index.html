Instructions

Use WASD to move, Shift to move up, Alt to move down, and mouse to look around.

You can change the number of spheres to be rendered (depending on what your GPU can handle because the BVH is of poor quality) and even toggle the ground to see how adding large triangles affects the BVH construction without the optimizations of Karras 2013!

To render the BVH, press "show BVH" and slide the "BVH depth" slider to see different levels of the BVH. What is rendered is all nodes exactly at the specified depth AND the leaf nodes if there are any. (It may be easier to see it sometimes in rasterization mode, uncheck "use raytracing" to see the BVH more clearly in wireframe).
